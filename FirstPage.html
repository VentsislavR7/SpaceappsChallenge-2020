United we Stand, Divided we Fall

These people have achieved great things that have helped many across the world. Due to that fact, the probability of them not getting harrassed based on their color as frequently as an ordinary person is greater. They're accepted by society and looked up to. This is how every single person of different color should be treated as, accepted and loved.

Martin Luther King

"Darkness cannot drive out darkness; only light can do that. Hate cannot drive out hate; only love can do that."

King donated all of his $54,123 in winnings from the Nobel Peace prize to the Civil Rights Movement.

Morgan Freeman

"I am going to stop calling you a white man and I'm going to ask you to stop calling me a black man."

Freeman has given support to the Mississippi Animal Rescue League, and he donated funds to help create a new research area at the Mississippi State College of Veterinary Medicine. He also helped set up a charity - the Grenada Relief Fund after two hurricanes ravaged the small Carribean island in 2004.

Whitney Houston

"I believe that children are our future. Teach them well and let them lead the way. Show them all the beauty they possess inside."

Houston formed the Whitney Houston Foundation for Children, an organization that cared for the homeless and children with cancer and AIDS. It eventually brought the singer one of her many awards for her humanitarian work.

Will Smith

"Throughout life people will make you mad, disrespect you and treat you bad. Let God deal with the things they do, cause hate in your heart will consume you too."

Will Smith is a well-known philanthropist as he frequently donates to multiple charities. Not only does he donate both money and his time to charities, but he also co-owns a charity foundation with his wife.
